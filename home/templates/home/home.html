Textbooky

Textbooky is a user-driven marketplace for students to buy, sell, and textbooks. Textbooky is unique in that it shows
book listings based off of the location of the user. This location-based service will specifically help users find convenient
deals in their area, which will make it easier to pick up and eliminate wasting money on shipping & handling costs. Textbooky
will be offered as a web application as well as an iOS mobile app. Log in below!
