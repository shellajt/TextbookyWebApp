Buy Books Locally

Textbooky allows you to locally buy and sell books. Now instead of having to go through the mundane process of an online service or the pricey path of bookstores, a book is ready to purchase from a simple few clicks.

Get Started

Why should I use Textbooky?

All Books are Local

This means that there are no wait times. Pick up the book whenever you want without the worry.

iOS + Web

Textbooky can be used in the comforts of your home or on-the-go using any iOS device

Ease of Use

Textbooky creates a clean and fast interface to allow a book transaction to go up instantly

No Extra Costs

No need to worry about the extra costs of shipping and handling like most online services.

Perfect for Students!

Textbooky provides services specific to university campuses such that students can find the books they want without having to pay the exorbitant prices from bookstores and other online services
